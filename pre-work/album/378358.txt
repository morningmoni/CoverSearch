introduction The Ultimate Remix Tata Young, the latest album is a collection of TATA YOUNG's popular hits. Remixed by well-known remixers, DJ Suketu, he has won the MTV IMMIES Award for "Best Remix Of The Year". He has included 3 unique remixes for the "Ready For Love" track in this collection.The popular Frankmusik who has remixed singles for Lady GaGa has created "Mission Is You" in Funky Robo-Pop groove with an electro flair. Out of Ireland comes Joe Echo who has co-written the mega hit "Celebration" for Madonna, he has given "Mission Is You" his magic touch in a flavorful techno dance beat vibe.Added to this collection are 3 popular singles from the "I Believe Album", Sexy Naughty Bitchy, I Believe and I Think Of You in their remixed versions. Included are Japanese language adaptations of some of these popular songs.
