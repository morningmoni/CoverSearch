introduction One Love by Tata Young is a Thai studio album released in March 2008. The first single from the album, "One Love", was released on Valentines Day. The second single, "Cause of Sadness", was later released. One Love is Tata Young's first studio album since her 2006 success Temperature Rising and her first all Thai album in several years.
